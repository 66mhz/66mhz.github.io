TerraSentia⌗

  • (https://www.nytimes.com/2020/02/13/science/farm-agriculture-robots.html)

Functions⌗

  • Measure plant height
  • Stem diameter
  • Leaf-area index
  • Stand count (num of live grain or fruit producing plants)

Dr.Chowdhary is working on this, and with its company EarthSense, which produces these robots.

In addition to plant height, TerraSentia can measure stem diameter, leaf-area index and “stand count” — the number of live grain- or fruit-producing plants — or all of those traits at once. And Dr. Chowdhary is working on adding even more traits, or phenotypes, to the list with the help of colleagues at EarthSense, a spinoff company that he created to manufacture more robots.
